Hello! I'm a junior UC Berkeley student who is passionate about software accessibility and the pursual of human rights; I hope to use what I've learned as a Computer Science major to develop technology that can advance public welfare and contribute to social good. I've also been exploring the world of Computer Graphics and 3D Design, where I aim to use my technical skills to automate and enhance graphical tools.

As a Project Leader for Blueprint, a student organization that develops software pro-bono for nonprofit groups, I am leading a project in partnership with Mee Panyar to build a mobile app. This app will support rural communities in the operation and maintenance of decentralized solar mini-grids that provide reliable, sustainable energy.

Additionally, I act as a class facilitator for the UC Berkeley Undergraduate Graphics Group, where I teach students how to navigate Autodesk Maya and perform each step of the 3D Animation pipeline.
